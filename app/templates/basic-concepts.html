Basic Concepts



Parts of a Card


Mana Cost

Found in the upper right corner, this is the cost you must pay to cast a particular spell. Each symbol requires one mana of that specific type. Symbols with a number or an ‘X’ inside are generic costs and can be paid for by mana of any type in any combination. (see #mana)


Type Line

Each card has a type. It may also have subtype or supertype. (see #Card Types)
